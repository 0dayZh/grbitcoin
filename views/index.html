Grbitcoin
A Globally Recognized Bitcoin address.

Introduction

There are thousands Bitcoin transcations every day, but you can not event remember your Bitcoin address. Find a way to connect to reality or face the reality. Why not send and receive your Bitcoin via email?

Grbitcoin gives a chance to you to do this, without abandoning decentralized Bitcoin system.

Developer Resources

Grbitcoin 'APIs' require no authentication, and are all based around simple HTTP GET requests.

Get a Bitcoin address

GET /bitcoins/:email

Response

Status: 200 OK
{
  "email": "0day.zh@gmail.com",
  "bitcoin_address": "19QT9EarJHQ97aV17TYaBgoyNhCqN1vthH"
}

Note: If the :email is not binded to Bitcoin address, there will be a 404 response

Get an email address

GET /emails/:bitcoin_address

Response

Status: 200 OK
{
  "email": "0day.zh@gmail.com",
  "bitcoin_address": "19QT9EarJHQ97aV17TYaBgoyNhCqN1vthH"
}

Note: If the :bitcoin_address is not binded to any email, there will be a 404 response
